About Me

Mary Rose O’Donnell is a student journalist from West Chester, PA. She is currently a junior at the University of Pittsburgh majoring in Information Science and English Writing (Nonfiction).

She has worked as a writer and editor for The Pitt News, the University of Pittsburgh’s student newspaper, since 2018 and has published several features on campus and city issues.

In addition to her work at The Pitt News, Mary Rose works as a First Year Mentor in the Office of New Student Programs at Pitt, where she helps current and incoming first year students adjust to college life.

She loves reading, writing, playing guitar, watching YouTube, listening to podcasts, and spending time with her cat and younger brother.
